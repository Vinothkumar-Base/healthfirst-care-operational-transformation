HealthFirst Care – Operational Transformation Project

🔎 Problem Statement

HealthFirst Care Hospital faced long wait times, overbooking, inefficient scheduling, and communication breakdowns.

🎯 Objective

Reduce patient wait times by 20% and improve resource utilization through process redesign and analytics.

📌 Key Deliverables
	•	Business Requirements Document (BRD)
	•	Stakeholder Analysis & Engagement Strategy
	•	Process Mapping (Current & Future State)
	•	Risk Management & Mitigation Plan
	•	Data Analysis & Dashboard Design

🛠 Tools Used
	•	Requirements documentation
	•	Stakeholder matrix
	•	Process flow modeling
	•	KPI design & visualization
	•	Risk assessment framework

📊 Outcome

Structured, end-to-end business analysis lifecycle simulation aligned to real-world healthcare transformation.
